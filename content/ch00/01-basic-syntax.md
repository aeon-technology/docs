## Bold, Italic

You can stylize your text in **bold**, _italic_, **_Both both and italic_**.

## Lists

Ordered list:

1. Section 1
   1. Item 1
   1. Item 2
      1. Sub item 1
      1. Sub item 2
   1. Item 3
2. Section 2
   1. Item 1

Unordered list:

- Section A
  - Item A
  - Item B
    - Sub item A
    - Sub item B
  - Item C
- Seciont B
  - Item A
